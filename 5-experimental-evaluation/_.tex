\subsection{Metrics and Indicators}
Speedup $S(N)$ for $N$ workers is an example of an evaluation metric defined as:

\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:example-equation}
  S(N) = \frac{T_{\text{serial}}}{T_{\text{parallel}}(N)}
\end{equation}

where $T_{\text{serial}}$ is the processing time of the serial run, and $T_{\text{parallel}}(N)$ is the time for the distributed run.
